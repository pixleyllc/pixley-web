Frequently Asked Questions
Q: How do I get verified on Pixley?
A: Go to your Profile settings and apply under “Verification.”
Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Use the “Forgot password?” link on the login page.
Q: How do I contact support?
A: Visit the Contact Support page.
Q: Can I delete my Pixley account?
A: Yes, in Account Settings you’ll find a delete option.
